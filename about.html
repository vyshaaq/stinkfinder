CAESAR-m

Thankan is Watching you.

Caesar-M (Comments Analysis and Evaluation Screening Algorithm for Rogue Detection in Malayalam) is designed to proficiently identify and manage rogue words, local slang, and slurs in Malayalam YouTube comments. Inspired by Thankan Chettan, a character known for his unfiltered language, this model aims to foster cleaner online discourse. Utilizing advanced tools like TensorFlow, Caesar-M will be trained on a substantial dataset of Malayalam text, targeting an accuracy rate of over 90%. Employing effective machine learning techniques and natural language processing methods, it ensures robust performance in real-world applications while understanding the nuances of the Malayalam dialect.

Advanced Sentiment Analysis

Accurate detection of sentiment in Malayalam text, capable of understanding nuances and context.

Real-time Comment Filtering

Instantly filters and moderates YouTube comments to maintain quality and relevance.

Multi-layered Language Understanding

Deep learning models that grasp various layers of language, enhancing the understanding of complex phrases.

Customizable Reporting

Flexible reporting tools that allow users to report and manage comments with ease.
